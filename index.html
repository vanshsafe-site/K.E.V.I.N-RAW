⌬ HOW TO USE K.E.V.I.N

🎤 Speak Mode

  • K.E.V.I.N speaks motivational messages directly to you
  • Tap the orb to activate, then say keep talking for a new message
  • Say thanks or exit to stop

👂 Listen Mode

  • K.E.V.I.N listens — vent freely without any judgment
  • Tap the orb to begin. Speak as long as you need
  • Say kevin stop when you're done talking
  • K.E.V.I.N will respond with a supportive message
  • Say exit to end the session

⚡ Consult Mode

  • Tap the orb and tell K.E.V.I.N what you're experiencing
  • e.g. "I have anxiety" or "I feel lonely"
  • K.E.V.I.N identifies the concern and provides tailored guidance
  • Over 100 mental health conditions recognized

⚠ Important Notes

  • Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser
  • Works best on Chrome or Edge (Web Speech API required)
  • K.E.V.I.N is a supportive AI, not a licensed medical professional
  • Tap ✕ button at any time to reset all modes
